## Description

An _AI Agent_ is software that can interact with its environment and perform actions to complete a defined goal.

At its core, this works by having a `large language model` operating in a loop in real time. _AI Agents_ can have access to tools, external services, or even other _AI Agents_ to help reach their goals.
